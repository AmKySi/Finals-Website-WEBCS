Community Guidelines

Do's

  • Share your fishing experiences and photos
  • Ask questions and help others learn
  • Respect fellow community members
  • Follow local fishing regulations

Don'ts

  • Share exact coordinates of sensitive spots
  • Post inappropriate or offensive content
  • Spam channels with unrelated content
  • Promote illegal fishing practices
