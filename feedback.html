Frequently Asked Questions

How is my feedback used?

We use your feedback to identify areas for improvement, prioritize new features, and enhance the overall community experience for all members.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Yes, your feedback is completely anonymous unless you choose to provide your contact information for follow-up questions.

Will I get a response?

If you provide your email, we may reach out for clarification or to let you know about improvements we've made based on your suggestions.

How often should I submit feedback?

We welcome feedback anytime! Whether you're new or a long-time member, your perspective is valuable to us.
